選出使用者的必修課:
select student.student_id,course.class_id,course.class_name from course inner join student on course.class = student.department where course.requirements='M';

課程的時間:
select course.class_name,course.class_id,time.day,time.sessions from course inner join time on course.class_id = time.class_id;

使用者的必修課:
select student.student_id,course.class_id,course.class_name,time.day,time.sessions from course,time,student where course.class = student.department and course.class_id = time.class_id and course.requirements='M' and student.student_id = "D0813011";

主要用來找使用者的必修課的學分
select distinct student.student_id,course.class_id,course.class_name,course.credits from course,time,student where course.class = student.department and course.class_id = time.class_id and course.requirements='M' and student.student_id = "D0813011";
